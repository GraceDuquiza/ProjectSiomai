Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Siomai Cart Sarap, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information when you visit our website. We respect your right to privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

We do not collect personal information unless you provide it voluntarily, such as through contacting us. However, we do collect non-personal information related to your website usage to improve our service and analyze traffic patterns.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information like your name, email address, or phone number unless you choose to provide it. However, we collect the following non-personally identifiable information through cookies:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • IP address
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Duration of time spent on our website

Use of Cookies

We use cookies to track website traffic, enhance your browsing experience, and improve the performance of our website. Cookies are small data files placed on your device to collect data about your activity on the site. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analyzing website traffic to improve content and functionality
  • Tracking website performance and user interactions

Cookies are also used by third-party services like Google Analytics to gather insights about website traffic. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may limit certain features of our website.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects non-personally identifiable data, such as:

  • Your IP address
  • Device and browser information
  • Pages visited and the time spent on those pages

We use this information solely for statistical purposes to improve the performance and usability of our website.

You can read more about Google Analytics and how it handles data collection by visiting their privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy.

How We Use the Information

The non-personally identifiable information we collect through cookies is used to analyze trends, improve website performance, and enhance the user experience. This data is strictly used for internal purposes only and is not shared with third parties unless required by law.

How We Protect Your Information

We take appropriate measures to protect the data we collect and prevent unauthorized access or misuse. However, as with all online services, we cannot guarantee absolute security of the data transmitted over the internet.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete any personal information that you have provided to us. You can also control the cookies used on this website by adjusting your browser settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to check this page regularly for any changes.
